No matter how long he keeps playing for, he will always be remembered as one of the greatest players of all time. 无论他还会踢多久，他都会被铭记为足球史上最伟大的球员之一。⭐ No matter how…：无论……｜be remembered as：被铭记为……
Well, it's really difficult to imagine that there's anything about Lionel Messi's life in Barcelona and with Barcelona Football Club that isn't very nice indeed. 很难想象，梅西在巴塞罗那、以及在巴萨俱乐部的生活中，还有什么是不美好的。⭐ it's difficult to imagine that…：很难想象……｜indeed：确实；的确（正式，C1）
However, he's never played in his native Argentina, he's never played a senior game in that country. 然而，他从未在自己的祖国阿根廷踢过职业一线队比赛。⭐ native：祖国的；出生地的（B2）｜senior game：一线队比赛

It may be that Messi decides that he does want to go back and play for his public, in his city, with his club. 也许梅西终究会决定回到自己的城市，为支持自己的球迷、自己的俱乐部而战。⭐ go back：回归｜play for：为……效力
Lionel Messi's name will forever be etched in football's history books. 莱昂内尔·梅西的名字将永远镌刻在足球史册之中。⭐ be etched in history：被载入史册（C2）｜history books：史册
A magician on the pitch, an inspiration off it, his legacy is immortal. 球场上，他是一位魔术师；球场外，他是一位激励无数人的榜样。他的传奇将永垂不朽。⭐ on the pitch / off it：球场上 / 球场外｜immortal：永恒的；不朽的（C2）
I tried to keep everything that I learned from when I started in the first team until today. 我一直努力保留自己从进入一线队至今所学到的一切。⭐ keep：（这里）保留；坚持｜first team：一线队
